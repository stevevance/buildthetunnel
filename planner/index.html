The trip planner will find the nearest rail station (existing, under construction, and proposed new CrossTowner) at your selected origin and destination and route a trip between them. These trips have been precomputed to ensure this website is super fast.

Made by Steven Vance, using the Star:Line Chicago CrossTowner map and synthetic schedule as input to Conveyal R5 trip-planning software. This tool is otherwise unaffiliated with Star:Line Chicago.

Your use of this planner is not logged: trips are computed in your browser from precomputed data, and the addresses you search are not stored. If you enter your email, it is saved only so Build the Tunnel can reach you. How this works →
